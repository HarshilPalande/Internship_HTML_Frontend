ROLE MODELS

MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni, known as "Captain Cool", led India to major cricket victories. His calm mindset, sharp strategies, and leadership make him an ideal role model for aspiring youth and athletes.

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam was a scientist and the 11th President of India. His humility, vision, and dedication to youth empowerment continue to inspire millions.

Indira Gandhi

India’s first woman Prime Minister, Indira Gandhi was known for her powerful leadership and bold decisions. She shaped India's modern political era with vision and strength.

Dr. Homi Bhabha

A pioneer of India's nuclear program, Dr. Homi Bhabha’s brilliance laid the foundation for scientific growth. He placed India on the global science map.

Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is the embodiment of divine wisdom, strategy, and compassion. His teachings in the Bhagavad Gita offer timeless life lessons on duty, righteousness, and devotion.
