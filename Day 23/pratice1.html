System Journey

Tracking our evolution step-by-step.

2015 — The Idea
A vision sparked — to bring automation, strategy, and marketing into a unified system that simplifies complexity and empowers brands.
2017 — First Launch
We launched with essential tools: email marketing, keyword tracking, and simplified dashboards — all in one place for digital teams.
2018 — Integrations
Expanded our ecosystem with integrations like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads, CRM tools, and CMS plugins to unify data flow.
2019 — Analytics Engine
Deployed real-time dashboards, KPI trackers, and campaign performance visualizers, helping marketers measure what matters.
2022 — AI Evolution
AI came to life — powering smart segmentation, dynamic post scheduling, predictive analytics, and custom campaign triggers.
2024 — Global Rollout
With multilingual capabilities and localization, the system is now active in 15+ countries, trusted by thousands of marketers worldwide.
