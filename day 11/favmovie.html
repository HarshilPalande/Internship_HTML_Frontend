My Favourite Movies

GOAT – Vijay Thalapathy

GOAT (Greatest Of All Time) is an upcoming Tamil science-fiction action drama featuring Vijay in dual roles. Directed by Venkat Prabhu, this film blends high-octane action with time travel elements. With a unique father-son storyline, cutting-edge visuals, and Anirudh’s music, GOAT is expected to be a game-changer in Tamil cinema.

Super 20

Super 20 is a compelling academic drama inspired by stories of real-life educators who transform lives. The movie highlights the struggles of brilliant students from humble backgrounds and the mentors who guide them. It's a mix of emotion, inspiration, and social reality, showcasing how knowledge can overcome all odds in the face of adversity.

Scam 1992

Based on the real events of India's 1992 stock market scam, this film uncovers the rise and fall of Harshad Mehta — a man who manipulated the financial system to become the 'Big Bull' of Dalal Street. The film paints a gripping portrait of ambition, media influence, loopholes in banking, and a scandal that changed India’s financial landscape forever.

Phir Hera Pheri 2

The sequel to the cult comedy, this movie continues the chaotic journey of Raju, Shyam, and Baburao. As they try their luck again with another get-rich-quick scheme, they encounter new gangsters, money troubles, and a ton of hilarious confusion. It's a laugh riot filled with nostalgia, iconic one-liners, and perfect comic timing by Akshay Kumar, Sunil Shetty, and Paresh Rawal.

Kesari Chp2 – The Jalianwala Bagh

A tribute to one of the darkest yet most heroic events in Indian history, Kesari Chapter 2 dramatizes the horrors of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre. With emotional storytelling, historical detail, and heart-wrenching visuals, this film honors the innocent lives lost and the strength of a freedom movement born from tragedy. A patriotic saga that stays etched in memory.
